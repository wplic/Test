International Business
To promote the company’s international strategy, and support business in Hong Kong, International Business serves as bridge between clients and international projects through analyzing overseas market, and providing services for QDII to expand into foreign markets.


■ GF’s services include:

Brokerage
GF provide clients with a variety of services in securities including equity securities traded funds in stock exchange, warrants, as well as derivatives on future indexes and bonds listed in HKEx. Outside of HKEx, GF offers services of brokerage in Shenzhen, Shanghai B share and major foreign exchanges, financing, and investment immigration.

Investment Banking
GF offers various corporate finance services including financial advisory, private offering of equity and debt products, sponsoring and underwriting, mergers & acquisitions and restructuring for institutions worldwide,

Asset Management
Management and operation utilize orthodox fund style, including fund issuing, operating, consultancy, account management, and investment consultancy.

Research Consulting
GF’s research covers Greater China Area and Hong Kong market, companies and investment suggestions through market research, investment strategy research, industry research and company research.

■ GF’s Advantages

Apart from Hong Kong, GF also provide services in America, Southeast Asia and other overseas market, which give comprehensive and suitable advices to clients on international horizon.
